Who am I?

I'm a student who never quit working toward a better understanding of computer science. After a poor grade in my Technology class in middle school, I decided my computer skills needed sharpening, and 7 years later, I've gained experience working on both programming and electronics. Practice makes perfect, and I know for certain I never would be where I am if not for my desire to strengthen my weaknesses.

My high school nurtured my interests in technology, providing me with the basic tools I would need to be prepared for college, and a career. But Computer Science wasn't my end-all; it was only the beginning for me. Cybersecurity would become my path, and my first experience in this field was through the Girls Go Cyberstart competition, where I first competed in a CTF, and realized solving challenges and puzzles to protect a system or application was what I wanted to do. Cybersecurity became my career path, and now, I am determined to become a Penetration-tester and serve the public by defending against malicious actors.
