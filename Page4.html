WiCyS Club Officer

Part of my freshman experience was finding a group where I belonged. While I discovered Cybersecurity through the TAMU Cyber club, the TAMU Women in Cybersecurity (WiCyS) club was where I found my friends. Becoming part of a close-knit community who I know has my back and will support me has been invaluable during my college experience. Just last year, I attended the Annual WiCyS Conference held in Denver, Colorado, where I learned the value of networking and the importance of diversity in thought.

Now, as a General Officer in TAMU WiCyS, it's my pleasure to help educate the new members on cybersecurity, encryption, and the importance of their own network for their career goals, and for their work-life balance.
