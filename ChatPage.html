Dangers of Cyberspace

Cyberspace presents numerous dangers, including:

  • Data breaches and cyberattacks
  • Identity theft and fraud
  • Online harassment and cyberbullying
  • Privacy invasion and surveillance
  • Spread of misinformation and fake news

Potential of AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) offers immense potential in various fields:

  • Healthcare: Diagnosis and treatment
  • Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading
  • Transportation: Autonomous vehicles
  • E-commerce: Personalized recommendations
  • Education: Personalized learning

However, it also raises concerns like job displacement and ethical dilemmas.
